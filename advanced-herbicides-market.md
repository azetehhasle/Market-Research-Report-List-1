<p><strong>The Advanced Herbicides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Translocated Herbicides</li><li>Contact Herbicides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced herbicides market is primarily categorized into translocated and contact herbicides. Translocated herbicides move throughout the plant after application, targeting the root system, making them effective for perennial weeds. In contrast, contact herbicides affect only the parts of the plant they directly touch, providing quick results but requiring targeted application to be effective. Both types play crucial roles in agricultural and landscape management, offering diverse strategies for weed control and enhancing crop productivity.</p></p>
