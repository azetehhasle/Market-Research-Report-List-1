<p><p>The sintered diamond saw blades market comprises two primary types: hot pressed sintered and cold press sintered. Hot pressed sintered blades are manufactured under high temperature and pressure, resulting in superior bonding and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty cutting applications. In contrast, cold pressed sintered blades are produced at lower temperatures, offering a cost-effective solution while maintaining decent performance for less intensive tasks. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, including construction, masonry, and stone processing.</p></p>
