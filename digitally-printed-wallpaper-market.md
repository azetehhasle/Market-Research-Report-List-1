<p><p>The digitally printed wallpaper market comprises three primary types: non-woven, pure paper, and vinyl-based wallpapers. Non-woven wallpaper, made from a blend of natural and synthetic fibers, offers durability and easy installation. Pure paper wallpaper, crafted from recycled paper, provides a classic aesthetic but may have lower moisture resistance. Vinyl-based wallpaper is known for its high durability, water resistance, and ease of cleaning, making it suitable for various environments. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and decorative needs.</p></p>
