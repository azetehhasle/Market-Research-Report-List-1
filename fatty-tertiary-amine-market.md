<p><p>The Fatty Tertiary Amine market can be categorized primarily into two processes: hydrogenation and distillation. Hydrogenation involves the chemical reaction where unsaturated fats are converted into saturated amines, enhancing their stability and performance in various applications. Distillation, on the other hand, separates components based on their boiling points, allowing for the purification of fatty tertiary amines. Both methods are essential for producing high-quality products used in surfactants, emulsifiers, and other industrial applications.</p></p>
