<p><p>The disposable surgical drapes market is divided into nonwoven and woven types. Nonwoven surgical drapes are made from synthetic fibers, offering benefits like lightweight, fluid resistance, and breathability, making them ideal for single-use applications. In contrast, woven surgical drapes are crafted from interlaced threads, providing durability and reusability, often used in more controlled environments. Both types are essential for infection control and maintaining a sterile field during surgical procedures, contributing to improved patient outcomes and safety.</p></p>
