<p><ul><li>Chute Selector</li><li>Belt Selector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color selector market types, such as chute and belt selectors, are essential in various industries for sorting and grading products based on color. Chute selectors use gravity to direct items down chutes, where cameras and sensors identify hues and separate materials accordingly. In contrast, belt selectors utilize moving conveyor belts to transport items while color detection systems analyze them on the fly. Both systems enhance efficiency and accuracy in quality control processes, optimizing production lines in food, recycling, and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
