<p><ul><li>Open Bearing</li><li>Sealed Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary tricone drill bits market comprises two primary types: open bearing and sealed bearing. Open bearing bits feature exposed bearings, allowing for better cooling and cleaning of debris, making them suitable for softer formations. In contrast, sealed bearing bits are designed with protective seals that prevent the ingress of contaminants, enhancing durability and performance in harsh conditions. Each type is selected based on the geological environment and operational requirements, impacting drilling efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
