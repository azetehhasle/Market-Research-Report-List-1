<p><p>Motor Starting Reactors are used to limit inrush current during motor startup, ensuring a smooth start and protecting equipment. The market primarily includes two types: Iron Core and Air Core reactors. Iron Core reactors utilize magnetic materials to enhance inductance and efficiency, making them suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, Air Core reactors rely on air as the magnetic medium, which allows for lighter construction and higher thermal performance, making them ideal for environments where weight and space are critical.</p></p>
