<p><p>The portable dialysis devices market comprises two primary types: peritoneal dialysis and hemodialysis. Peritoneal dialysis involves introducing a dialysis solution into the abdominal cavity, allowing waste removal through the peritoneal membrane, offering greater flexibility and home-use options. Hemodialysis, on the other hand, filters blood externally through a machine, requiring more consistent access to healthcare facilities. Both types emphasize patient convenience, mobility, and improved quality of life, catering to the increasing demand for home and portable treatment solutions.</p></p>
