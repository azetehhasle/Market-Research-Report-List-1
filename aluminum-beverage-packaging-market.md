<p><ul><li>DRD (Draw and Redraw) Cans</li><li>DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum beverage packaging market includes various types, notably DRD (Draw and Redraw) and DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) cans. DRD cans are produced through a single-step process, resulting in a more lightweight and cost-effective option, ideal for carbonated drinks. In contrast, DWI cans undergo an additional ironing step, providing enhanced strength and forming capabilities, making them suitable for thicker beverages or products requiring a more robust structure. Both types emphasize efficiency and sustainability in packaging solutions.</p></p>
