<p><ul><li>Internal Tool Post Grinder</li><li>External Tool Post Grinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tool post grinder market consists of two primary types: internal and external tool post grinders. Internal tool post grinders are designed to achieve precise internal finishes, making them ideal for machining cylindrical components with intricate internal geometries. In contrast, external tool post grinders focus on refining the surfaces of external workpieces, suitable for shaping and smoothing the outer surfaces of various materials. Both types enhance machining capabilities, providing versatility and improved accuracy in metalworking applications.</p></p>
