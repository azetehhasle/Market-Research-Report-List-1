<p><p>The progesterone test kit market includes various testing methodologies, prominently luminescence immunoassay and time-resolved analysis. Time-resolved analysis, on the other hand, employs time-based measurements of emitted signals to enhance detection accuracy and reduce background interference. Both methods are widely used in clinical diagnostics, fertility assessments, and hormone monitoring, catering to the growing demand for precise and efficient hormonal testing solutions.</p></p>
