<p><strong>The ISO 2858 Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magdrive Pumps</li><li>Sealed Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ISO 2858 pumps market is primarily segmented into two types: magdrive pumps and sealed pumps. Magdrive pumps utilize a magnetic coupling, eliminating the need for shaft seals, resulting in lower leakage and maintenance needs, ideal for handling hazardous materials. In contrast, sealed pumps use traditional mechanical seals to contain fluid within the system, offering reliability and efficiency for a wide range of applications. Each type serves distinct industries based on operational requirements, safety, and process efficiency.</p></p>
