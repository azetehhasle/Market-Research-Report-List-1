<p><ul><li>Through Hole Type</li><li>SMD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog potentiometers market is primarily segmented into two types: Through Hole Type and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type. Through Hole potentiometers feature leads that pass through a circuit board, providing robust mechanical stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, SMD potentiometers are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of PCBs, enabling compact designs and automated assembly processes. Both types cater to various industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation, each with unique requirements for size, durability, and performance.</p></p>
