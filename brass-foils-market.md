<p><p>The brass foils market includes distinct types based on copper quality and composition. Oxygen-Free High Conductivity Copper (OFHC) offers superior conductivity and resistance to oxidation, making it ideal for electronics. Electrolytic Tough Pitch (ETP) Copper is commonly used due to its balance of conductivity and cost-effectiveness. Deoxidized High Phosphorous (DHP) Copper is known for its excellent strength and resistance to corrosion, while Deoxidized Low Phosphorous (DLP) Copper provides improved machinability and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
