<p><p>The reflective air bags market primarily consists of two types: void fill and cushioning. Void fill air bags are designed to occupy empty spaces in packages, preventing movement during transit and enhancing product safety. Cushioning air bags, on the other hand, provide shock absorption and impact protection for fragile items. Both types use air to create a lightweight yet effective barrier against damage, making them popular in packaging solutions for various industries that require reliable product protection during shipping.</p></p>
