<p><p>The High Throughput Pipetting market encompasses two primary types: manual and electric pipetting systems. Manual pipetting systems rely on human dexterity to transfer liquids, offering flexibility and low initial costs, but they can be time-consuming and labor-intensive. Electric pipetting systems, on the other hand, automate liquid handling, increasing precision and throughput while reducing human error. These systems cater to laboratories requiring efficiency in tasks such as sample preparation, drug discovery, and genomics, driving advancements in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
