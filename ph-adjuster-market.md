<p><p>The pH adjuster market comprises several key types: adjuvants, soil treatments, aglime, and gypsum. Adjuvants enhance the effectiveness of fertilizers and pesticides by improving their performance in varying pH conditions. Soil treatments involve applying substances to modify soil pH, promoting nutrient availability and crop health. Aglime, primarily calcium carbonate, helps raise acidic soil pH, improving agricultural productivity. Gypsum, or calcium sulfate, also adjusts soil pH while enhancing soil structure and nutrient absorption, crucial for sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
