<p><p>Holographic sights are categorized into two main types: open type and tube type. Open type holographic sights feature a non-enclosed design, allowing for a wider field of view, making target acquisition faster and easier. These sights are typically lighter and more compact. In contrast, tube type holographic sights are housed in a cylindrical body, providing added protection against environmental factors and unfavorable lighting conditions. This design often aids in more precise aiming, appealing to tactical and competitive shooters alike.</p></p>
