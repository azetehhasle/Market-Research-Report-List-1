<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water electrolysis equipment involves two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize an alkaline solution, typically potassium hydroxide, to facilitate the electrolysis process, offering cost-effectiveness and durability for large-scale hydrogen production. PEM electrolyzers, on the other hand, utilize a solid polymer membrane for ion conduction, allowing for higher purity hydrogen generation and faster response times. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in sustainable energy and hydrogen fuel production.</p></p>
