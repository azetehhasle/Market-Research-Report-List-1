<p><p>The KW-Class High Power Fiber Lasers market is segmented into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring consistent power output, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers deliver short bursts of energy, suitable for precision tasks like marking and micro-machining. This differentiation allows industries to select the appropriate technology based on specific processing needs and material types.</p></p>
