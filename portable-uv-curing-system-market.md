<p><p>The Portable UV Curing System market is categorized into two primary types: Spot-Type and Line-Type. Spot-Type systems are designed for targeted curing applications, allowing users to focus ultraviolet light on specific areas for precise curing, making them ideal for small or intricate tasks. In contrast, Line-Type systems offer a continuous curing solution, utilizing a broader UV light beam suitable for larger surfaces or high-speed production lines, enabling efficient processing of substantial materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
