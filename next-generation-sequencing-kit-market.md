In contrast, long-read sequencing can produce sequences exceeding 10,000 base pairs, allowing for the analysis of complex genomic regions and structural variations. Both types cater to diverse research needs, including genomics, epigenomics, and transcriptomics, shaping modern molecular biology.</p></p>
