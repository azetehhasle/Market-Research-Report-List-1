<p><p>The laboratory evaporator market comprises various types designed for different applications. Nitrogen evaporators utilize nitrogen gas to efficiently remove solvents at low temperatures, minimizing sample degradation. Vacuum evaporators operate under reduced pressure, allowing for rapid vaporization of liquids without high temperatures. Rotary evaporators combine rotation and heat to enhance evaporation efficiency, commonly used in chemical synthesis and distillation processes. Additionally, other types include film evaporators and centrifugal evaporators, catering to specialized needs in laboratory environments.</p></p>
