<p><p>The Two Factor Authentication (2FA) Display Card market encompasses two main types: One Time Password (OTP) Display Cards and OTP & Biometric Display Cards. OTP Display Cards generate unique codes for enhanced security during logins, ensuring that only authorized users can access sensitive information. In contrast, OTP & Biometric Display Cards combine OTP functionality with biometric verification, such as fingerprint or facial recognition, providing an additional layer of protection. Both types cater to growing security needs across various sectors.</p></p>
