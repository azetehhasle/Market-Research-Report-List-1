<p><strong>The Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (VELCB)</li><li>Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (CELCB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (ELCB) market comprises two primary types: Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (VELCB) and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (CELCB). VELCBs monitor the voltage level in the circuit and trip when a voltage leakage to the ground is detected, enhancing protection against electrical shocks. In contrast, CELCBs focus on the detection of leakage currents by comparing the live and neutral currents, disconnecting power when an imbalance occurs, thereby ensuring safety against potential hazards.</p></p>
