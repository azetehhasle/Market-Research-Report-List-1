<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) market is divided into two main types: Closed MRI Systems and Open MRI Systems. Closed MRI Systems feature a cylindrical design, offering high-resolution images and faster scanning times, making them ideal for detailed diagnostics. Conversely, Open MRI Systems have a more open architecture, improving patient comfort and accommodating those with claustrophobia or larger body sizes, though they generally provide lower image quality and longer scan durations. Both types cater to diverse patient needs and clinical requirements.</p></p>
