<p><strong>The Nuclear Imaging Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PET</li><li>SPECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear imaging devices primarily consist of Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). PET utilizes radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes, making it invaluable in oncology, cardiology, and neurology for detecting abnormalities. SPECT, on the other hand, provides functional imaging of organs by capturing gamma rays emitted from radioactive materials, often used to assess heart conditions and brain disorders. Both modalities enhance diagnostic capabilities, guiding treatment decisions and improving patient outcomes in various medical fields.</p></p>
