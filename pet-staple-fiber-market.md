<p><ul><li>Virgin PET Staple Fiber</li><li>Recycled PET Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET staple fiber market consists of two main types: virgin PET staple fiber and recycled PET staple fiber. Virgin PET staple fiber is produced from newly manufactured polyethylene terephthalate resin, offering high quality and durability, making it ideal for applications in textiles and industrial uses. Conversely, recycled PET staple fiber is derived from post-consumer or post-industrial PET waste, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact. It is often used in applications where cost-effectiveness and recycling are prioritized, such as insulation and filling materials.</p></p>
