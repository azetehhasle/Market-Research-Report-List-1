<p><p>The water-jet plastics cutting machine market is primarily divided into two types: abrasive water-jet and pure water-jet cutting. Abrasive water-jet machines utilize a mixture of water and abrasive materials to cut through hard plastics, making them suitable for thicker or more complex materials. In contrast, pure water-jet machines use only high-pressure water to slice through softer plastics and delicate materials. Each type caters to specific applications, with abrasive systems offering versatility and pure systems emphasizing precision and cleanliness.</p></p>
