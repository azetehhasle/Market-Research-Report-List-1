<p><p>The Public Address and General Alarm (PAGA) system market comprises two main types: Traditional Pressure Broadcasting and Network Broadcasting Systems. Traditional Pressure Broadcasting utilizes individual speakers connected through pressure-resistant cables to deliver announcements and alarms, typically in industrial settings. In contrast, Network Broadcasting Systems leverage digital technology and networks to disseminate audio messages, enabling integration with other systems and offering advanced functionalities like remote access and control. Both types cater to diverse safety and communication needs across various industries.</p></p>
