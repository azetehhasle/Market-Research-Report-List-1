<p><strong>The Chemical Release Agent Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Mold Releases</li><li>External Mold Releases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical release agent market encompasses both internal and external mold releases, essential for various manufacturing processes. Internal mold releases are incorporated into the resin or substrate, facilitating the separation of the final product from the mold without damaging its surface. External mold releases, applied directly to the mold surface, prevent adhesion between the mold and the workpiece. Both types enhance production efficiency, reduce cycle times, and improve surface quality in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods manufacturing.</p></p>
