<p><ul><li>Internal Surface Coatings</li><li>External Surface Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bonded Coatings market is divided into two primary types: Internal Surface Coatings and External Surface Coatings. Internal Surface Coatings are designed to enhance the durability and efficiency of components within systems, such as pipes and tanks, by minimizing corrosion and wear. In contrast, External Surface Coatings focus on protecting the outer surfaces of structures and equipment from environmental factors like moisture and UV exposure, ensuring longevity and maintaining aesthetic appeal. Both types are critical for industrial applications.</p></p>
