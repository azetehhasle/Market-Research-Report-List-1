<p><p>The waterproof intercom market is categorized into two main types: wall-mounted intercoms and flush-mount intercoms. In contrast, flush-mount intercoms are embedded into walls, offering a sleeker appearance and protecting the device from environmental elements. Both types are essential for effective communication in areas prone to exposure, ensuring durability and reliability in harsh conditions.</p></p>
