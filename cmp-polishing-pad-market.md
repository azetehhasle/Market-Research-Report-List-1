<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP polishing pad market is divided into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for aggressive material removal, typically used for harder substrates like silicon or metal layers, offering durability and efficient performance. In contrast, soft CMP pads provide a gentler touch, ideal for polishing delicate surfaces or thin films. Their cushioning effect minimizes scratching and defects, making them suitable for applications requiring a higher level of precision and surface quality.</p></p>
