<p><p>The watch market can be categorized into three main types: quartz watches, mechanical watches, and others. Quartz watches, known for their precision and low maintenance, utilize a battery to power the movement. Mechanical watches, prized for their craftsmanship, rely on intricate gear systems and manual winding or automatic movements. The “others” category encompasses smartwatches and hybrid models, combining traditional style with modern technology, appealing to tech-savvy consumers seeking multifunctionality alongside classic design. Each type caters to diverse preferences and uses.</p></p>
